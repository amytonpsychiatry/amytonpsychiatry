Introductory Phone Call

We will first connect over the phone so I can get a sense of what you seek if we could be a good fit.

Initial Consultation

After this, we will spend two sessions together where I'll get to learn more about you and your history. I'll share my impressions of what I think could be going on (a diagnosis), which we will discuss about, and treatment recommendations based on your needs, which can include medications and/ or individual therapy with me or another provider.

Medication Management

When warranted, I use evidence-based psychopharmacology to guide medication recommendations, which we will collaborate together to see if they might fit your goals and lifestyle. I can continue regimens that you know work for you in the past or further refine them. I am comfortable with complex medication managment.
